What Fauna does

Your animal is communicating.
Fauna helps you read it.

Audio insight

Record a vocalization and Fauna reads its length and loudness, together with the context you add, into a first-person take on what your animal may be expressing.

Video analysis

Share up to ten seconds of video. Fauna examines movement, posture, and body-language progression across the frames in sequence.

Pattern detection

Over time, Fauna builds a behavioral baseline unique to your animal and surfaces shifts worth a closer look.

Health observations

AI-generated observations based on behavioral patterns — kept on your device to review or share with your veterinarian. Not a diagnosis.

Behavioral timeline

Every observation logged and searchable — a complete behavioral record across the lifetime of your animal.

Built for professionals

Designed to scale to vet clinics, kennels, grooming salons, research centers, and aquariums as team features roll out.
