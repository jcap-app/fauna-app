WHAT FAUNA DOES

YOUR ANIMAL IS TALKING.
FAUNA TRANSLATES.

🎙
AUDIO TRANSLATION
Record vocalizations and Fauna decodes the acoustic profile — volume, duration, intensity — into first-person behavioral insight.
📹
VIDEO ANALYSIS
Upload up to 10 seconds of video. Fauna analyzes movement, posture, and body language frame by frame.
🔬
PATTERN DETECTION
Over time, Fauna builds a behavioral baseline unique to your animal and flags emerging health or behavioral concerns.
⚠️
HEALTH ALERTS
AI-generated health observations based on behavioral patterns. Permanent audit trail you can share with your vet.
📋
BEHAVIORAL TIMELINE
Every observation logged and searchable. Build a complete behavioral record over the lifetime of your animal.
🏢
BUILT FOR PROFESSIONALS
Vet clinics, kennels, grooming salons, research centers, aquariums — Fauna scales to your team and your animal count.
