Frequently asked questions

How accurate is Fauna’s AI?

Fauna uses Claude, Anthropic’s advanced AI, drawing on broad general knowledge that includes behavioral science. Results improve with more context — detailed descriptions, photos, and video all help. Your star ratings are saved and included as context for future analyses of that animal. Fauna is an informational tool, not a replacement for professional veterinary advice, and its output may be inaccurate.

Why isn’t my data syncing across devices?

Fauna stores all data locally on your device by design — this protects your privacy and keeps your animal’s data yours. Cloud sync is not currently available, so your data lives on the device where you installed the app.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. On your iPhone go to Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions, find Fauna, and tap Cancel Subscription. Your subscription stays active until the end of the current billing period.

What are health alerts?

Health alerts are AI-generated observations based on behavioral patterns detected across your logs. They are not medical diagnoses. A health alert means Fauna noticed something that may be worth discussing with your veterinarian — not confirmation that your animal is ill.

Can I delete health alerts?

Health alerts are kept on your device as a behavioral record you can review or share with your veterinarian. They are never sent to external servers. You can change a record’s status, and you can remove your data at any time by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app.

How does video translation work?

Fauna extracts frames from your video at about two frames per second and sends them to Claude for sequential analysis of movement, posture changes, and body-language progression. Video is available on the Plus and Premium plans.

What species does Fauna support?

Fauna is built for all animal species. Analysis quality is best for common domestic species (dogs, cats, rabbits) and improves with the specific context you provide about your animal.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your animal data is stored locally on your device. The only data sent externally is what’s needed for AI analysis (behavior descriptions, photos, video frames — and for audio, on-device metadata only, not the raw recording), and only when you request an interpretation. We do not keep a server copy; the AI processor retains data under its own policy. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Is Fauna for businesses too?

Yes. Fauna’s Professional plan is designed for vet clinics, kennels, grooming salons, research centers, aquariums, zoos, and trainers, with higher animal limits and professional features rolling out over time. Contact us to learn more.
