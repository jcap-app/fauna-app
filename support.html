FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

HOW ACCURATE IS FAUNA'S AI TRANSLATION?

Fauna uses Claude, Anthropic's advanced AI, trained on extensive behavioral science literature. Accuracy improves with more context — detailed descriptions, photos, and audio all help. Rate translations using the star system to help Fauna learn your animal's patterns over time. Fauna is an informational tool, not a replacement for professional veterinary advice.

WHY ISN'T MY DATA SYNCING ACROSS DEVICES?

Fauna stores all data locally on your device by design — this protects your privacy and keeps your animal's data yours. Cloud sync is not currently available. Your data lives on the device where you installed the app.

HOW DO I CANCEL MY SUBSCRIPTION?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Fauna, and tap Cancel Subscription. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period.

WHAT ARE HEALTH ALERTS?

Health alerts are AI-generated observations based on behavioral patterns detected across your logs. They are not medical diagnoses. If you receive a health alert, it means Fauna has detected something worth discussing with your veterinarian — it is not confirmation that your animal is ill.

WHY CAN'T I DELETE HEALTH ALERTS?

Health alert records are permanently stored by design. They form an audit trail of behavioral health observations that can be valuable for your veterinarian. You can change their status (acknowledged, resolved, dismissed) but the records themselves are preserved for your animal's safety.

HOW DOES VIDEO TRANSLATION WORK?

Fauna extracts frames from your video at 2 frames per second and sends them to Claude AI for sequential analysis. The AI examines movement, posture changes, and body language progression across the frames. Video translation is available on Fauna+ and Premium plans.

WHAT SPECIES DOES FAUNA SUPPORT?

Fauna supports all animal species. The AI is trained on broad behavioral science literature covering mammals, birds, reptiles, fish, and more. Analysis quality is best for common domestic species (dogs, cats, rabbits) and improves with specific context you provide about your animal.

IS MY DATA PRIVATE?

Yes. All your animal data is stored locally on your device. The only data sent externally is what's needed for AI analysis (behavior descriptions, photos, audio, video frames) — and only when you request a translation. We do not store this data after the response is returned. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

FAUNA IS FOR BUSINESSES TOO?

Yes. Fauna's Professional plan is built for vet clinics, kennels, grooming salons, research centers, aquariums, zoos, and trainers. It supports unlimited animals, team access, bulk logging, and professional export. Contact us to learn more about Professional pricing.
