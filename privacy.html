Privacy Policy

Effective & last updated · June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal data is collected, used, and handled when you use the Fauna mobile application (“Fauna” or the “App”).

1. Who we are (Data Controller)

The data controller responsible for your personal data is Jason Capostagno, an individual sole proprietor based in New York, USA (to be replaced with “Fauna LLC” once the LLC is formed).

  • Contact / data-protection requests: fauna.app.support@gmail.com
  • Postal address: available on request at the email above

We do not have an establishment in the European Union and have not appointed an EU/UK representative under Article 27 GDPR; given the nature and scale of processing we rely on the Article 27(2) derogation where applicable.

2. Summary (the short version)

  • Your logs, photos, audio, video, and notes are stored locally on your device. We do not operate a database that holds your content.
  • To generate an interpretation, the content you submit is sent off your device to a third-party AI provider (Anthropic) via a processing proxy (hosted on Cloudflare). This involves a transfer to the United States.
  • We do not sell your personal data and do not use it for third-party advertising.
  • We do not currently pool or aggregate your observations into any community dataset. If we ever offer that, it will be opt-in and this policy will be updated first.
  • Fauna does not provide veterinary or medical advice. See our Terms of Service.

3. What data we process

a) Content you create (“Observation Content”). Free-text behavior descriptions, photos, video recordings, audio recordings, animal profile details (name, species, breed, age, weight, notes, routine), and accuracy ratings you give. This is stored locally on your device.

When you request an interpretation, what is sent off your device depends on the type:

  • Text, photos, and video are transmitted to our AI processor to generate the interpretation.
  • Audio is processed on your device into rough metadata only (approximate recording length and a loudness estimate derived from file size) — the raw audio file is not transmitted to the AI processor; it stays on your device unless you delete it. Only that metadata and any text context you add are sent.

b) Device and app data. App settings, subscription/entitlement state, and locally stored counters (e.g. monthly log counts). Subscription status is managed through Apple’s App Store / Google Play and your RevenueCat-validated entitlement; we do not receive your full payment-card details.

c) Push notification token (if you enable notifications) — used to deliver reminders/alerts you opted into.

d) Diagnostic data — crash and basic performance data as provided by the app-store platforms.

We do not intentionally collect special-category data. Because Observation Content is free text, please do not enter sensitive personal information about identifiable people.

5. AI processing and third parties

To generate interpretations, the Observation Content you submit (text, photos, and video — and for audio, only on-device-derived metadata plus your text context, not the raw audio file) is transmitted from your device to:

  • Cloudflare, Inc. — hosts the processing proxy through which requests pass. See cloudflare.com/privacypolicy
  • Anthropic, PBC — the AI provider that generates the interpretation. See anthropic.com/legal/privacy

We pass your content to these providers solely to produce the output you requested. We do not control how these providers log, cache, or retain data on their own systems; please review their policies. We instruct our providers not to use submitted content to train their models where such controls are available to us, but you should not rely on this for confidential material. We do not use any other analytics SDKs or advertising networks to collect your Observation Content.

6. International data transfers

We are in the United States, and our AI processor (Anthropic) and proxy host (Cloudflare) process data in the United States and other countries. If you are in the EEA, the UK, or Switzerland, your data is therefore transferred outside your home region. Where required, such transfers are made under appropriate safeguards, including the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and/or the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework and its UK extension, as offered by the relevant provider. Request information about the safeguards at fauna.app.support@gmail.com.

7. Storage and retention

  • Observation Content stays on your device until you delete it (delete a log, an animal, or use “Clear All” in Settings) or uninstall the app. We do not retain a server copy.
  • Content sent to our AI processor for an interpretation is retained by that processor under their retention policies (Section 5), not ours.
  • Subscription/entitlement records are retained by Apple/Google/RevenueCat per their policies and our need to honor your purchases.

Because content lives on your device, uninstalling the app or using the in-app delete tools is the primary way to erase it.

8. Your rights

EEA / UK / Switzerland (GDPR): access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and the right to withdraw consent. Because most Observation Content is stored locally, you can exercise access and erasure directly in the app. For any data we or our processors hold, contact fauna.app.support@gmail.com. You may also lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

California (CCPA/CPRA): the right to know, delete, correct, and to opt out of “sale”/“sharing.” We do not sell or share your personal information as defined under the CPRA, and we do not process it for cross-context behavioral advertising. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

To make a request, email fauna.app.support@gmail.com; we respond within the timeframe required by law (generally one month under GDPR; 45 days under CCPA, extendable).

9. Children

Fauna is not directed to children. You must be at least 13 years old (16 in the EEA/UK) to use Fauna. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children below the applicable age. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact fauna.app.support@gmail.com and we will delete it.

10. Security

We use HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit and rely on platform device security for data at rest on your device. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “last updated” date and, where appropriate, an in-app notice. Continued use after an update constitutes acceptance where permitted by law.

12. Contact

Questions or requests: fauna.app.support@gmail.com. Postal address available on request.
